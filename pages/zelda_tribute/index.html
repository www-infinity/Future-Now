📜 Timeline of Hyrule

Key moments across the Zelda timeline.

The Creation — The Golden Goddesses

Din, Nayru, and Farore descend and shape the world. They leave the Triforce at the Sacred Realm before ascending to the heavens.

Skyward Sword — The First Hero

Link and Zelda fight Demise above the clouds of Skyloft, sowing the seed of an eternal cycle of reincarnation.

Ocarina of Time — The Hero of Time

Young Link draws the Master Sword, is sealed for seven years, and must reclaim Hyrule from Ganondorf's iron grip.

Twilight Princess — Twilight Invasion

Zant shatters the Mirror of Twilight. Wolf Link and Midna traverse the shadow realm to restore light to Hyrule.

Breath of the Wild — The Great Calamity

Calamity Ganon strikes. Link awakens 100 years later with no memory, facing the ruins of a kingdom lost to ancient evil.

Tears of the Kingdom — The Upheaval

Ancient darkness rises from beneath Hyrule Castle. Link must rebuild a fractured world and confront the Demon King.
